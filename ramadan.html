Preparing for a Season of Giving with "Iftar Saem"

Misr El Kheir begins the Ramadan season by packing and distributing "Iftar Saem" boxes, aiming to support families in need with essential food items during the holy month.

About the Iftar Saem Campaign

The "Iftar Saem" campaign is an initiative to provide meals for those fasting during Ramadan. Misr El Kheir is committed to supporting low-income families by distributing essential food boxes to ensure everyone can break their fast with a nutritious meal.

Highlights of the Iftar Saem Campaign

Food Box Preparation

Teams of volunteers and staff work together to pack Ramadan boxes filled with rice, lentils, pasta, oil, and other essentials to provide balanced meals for families.

Community Engagement

Misr El Kheir engages local communities in the campaign, offering individuals a chance to volunteer, donate, or sponsor boxes for families in need.

Nationwide Distribution

Distribution efforts span across cities and villages, ensuring that food boxes reach remote areas and families who rely on these resources during Ramadan.

Impact Stories

Fatima’s Gratitude: Fatima, a mother of three, expressed heartfelt thanks for the food box that made a difference during the holy month.

Volunteering Experience: Ahmed shared how volunteering in the campaign brought him closer to his community and gave him a sense of fulfillment.
