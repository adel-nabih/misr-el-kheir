Empowering Communities through Economic Growth

Misr El Kheir supports economic empowerment programs, benefiting thousands of individuals through microloans, job training, and small business development.

About Economic Empowerment Initiatives

Misr El Kheir’s economic empowerment programs are designed to create long-term economic independence for individuals in need. By offering financial resources, skills training, and business development opportunities, we help participants achieve sustainable livelihoods.

Key Economic Empowerment Programs

Microloan Initiatives

Misr El Kheir’s microloan program provides financial assistance to individuals looking to start small businesses. These loans help them grow their ventures, create jobs, and contribute to local economies.

Job Training Programs

Focused on equipping individuals with in-demand skills, the job training programs prepare people for employment opportunities. They offer workshops in areas like technology, trades, and customer service.

Small Business Support

Through education and mentorship, Misr El Kheir supports aspiring entrepreneurs by helping them launch and sustain their small businesses. The program offers resources like business management training and access to networks.

Success Stories

Hassan’s Story: Hassan used a microloan to open a tailoring business, which now provides jobs to four people in his community.

Layla’s Journey: Layla completed a job training program and was hired by a local company, greatly improving her family’s income.
