Empowering 2,500 Female Breadwinners

Through sustainable projects, Misr El Kheir has impacted the lives of over 2,500 female breadwinners across various regions, providing them with stability, growth, and resilience.

About the Initiative

Misr El Kheir's program focuses on empowering women by supporting them with resources, training, and opportunities to start their own income-generating projects. These projects aim to improve their financial independence and contribute to their communities' growth.


Impact of Empowerment Projects

Project Number of Beneficiaries Type of Support
Handicraft Training and Employment 800+ Skill training and job placement in traditional crafts
Small-Scale Farming 600+ Access to land, seeds, and agricultural tools
Micro-Enterprises in Food Production 500+ Provision of equipment for baking, cooking, and distribution
Local Market Stalls 300+ Training and resources for managing small businesses
Educational Support for Women Entrepreneurs 300+ Workshops on financial literacy and business management

Success Stories

Amina’s Story: Amina from Upper Egypt received training in handcrafting and now runs a small business that supports her family and provides employment for others in her community.

Fatima’s Journey: Fatima was able to start a livestock business, which has become a sustainable source of income for her household, ensuring her children can attend school regularly.
