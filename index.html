Misr El Kheir

Misr El Kheir Foundation is a non-profit organization dedicated to improving the lives of Egyptians through various programs in education, healthcare, social support, and economic empowerment. Join us in making a difference.

Support Families in Palestine

Your contributions can make a huge difference in the lives of those affected by the crisis. Join us in providing essential aid and support to families in need.

Save Your Environment

Take a stand and help us protect our environment. Join our team as we work to combat pollution in Egypt's agriculture sector. Every action counts.

Make Your Ramadan Count

Help those less fortunate than you this Ramadan. During this holy month, help Misr El Kheir prepare iftar boxes for those in need.
