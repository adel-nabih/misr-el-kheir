Student Health Program

Our Mission:

The Student Health Program aims to ensure the well-being of students by providing comprehensive health services, including mental health support, medical checkups, and access to necessary treatments. We believe that healthy students are better able to succeed academically and personally.


Key Features:

  • Medical Services: Access to regular health checkups, immunizations, and treatment for common illnesses.
  • Mental Health Support: Counseling and resources to help students manage stress, anxiety, and other mental health challenges.
  • Health Education: Workshops and resources on healthy living, nutrition, and exercise.
  • Emergency Response: 24/7 support for students in case of medical emergencies or urgent health issues.

Impact:

By providing students with access to medical care and health education, the Student Health Program helps reduce absenteeism, improves academic performance, and supports the overall well-being of students. A healthy student is a more engaged and successful student.


Success Stories:

  • Mental Health Initiative: A student dealing with anxiety was able to get counseling through the program, leading to improved academic performance and personal growth.
  • Health Screening in Rural Areas: Health screenings conducted in rural schools helped identify and treat medical conditions that had previously gone unnoticed, improving student health and attendance.

Our goal is to provide every student with the support and care they need to thrive, both academically and in life.
