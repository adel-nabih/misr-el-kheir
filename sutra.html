Providing Shelter and Dignity: The Sutra Project

Alwaleed Philanthropies "Global" and Misr El Kheir Foundation collaborate to launch Phase 6 of the Sutra Project, providing 10,000 housing units to vulnerable families across Egypt.

About the Sutra Project

The Sutra Project aims to improve living conditions for Egypt’s vulnerable families by offering stable housing solutions. By providing secure and sustainable housing units, the project enhances the quality of life for thousands, creating a lasting impact on communities across Egypt.

Project Highlights

Phase 6: 10,000 New Homes

In this new phase, the Sutra Project will provide 10,000 additional housing units, continuing its mission to empower vulnerable families. The goal is to improve access to safe, dignified housing and strengthen community ties.

Partnership for a Stronger Future

The partnership between Alwaleed Philanthropies "Global" and Misr El Kheir Foundation combines resources and expertise to address housing insecurities in Egypt. Together, they focus on creating long-term, sustainable housing solutions for families in need.

Empowering Families

Beyond housing, the Sutra Project offers support services to help families maintain their homes, fostering independence and stability in their communities.

Success Stories

Ayman’s New Start: Ayman and his family were given a new home, which provided a stable foundation for their future. "Thanks to Sutra, I can finally offer my children a secure and safe environment," he shares.

Sarah’s Dream Realized: For Sarah, moving into her new home meant more than just shelter—it meant dignity and hope for a better life. "This home has changed everything for us," she says.
