Cairo Festival City Mall and Misr El Kheir Foundation Deliver Food Supply Boxes

Supporting Egyptian communities, Cairo Festival City Mall has partnered with Misr El Kheir Foundation to distribute thousands of food supply boxes, helping families in need across the region.

About the Food Supply Box Initiative

This initiative, a collaboration between Cairo Festival City Mall and Misr El Kheir Foundation, is focused on providing essential food items to underserved communities in Egypt. These boxes aim to support families with nutritious staples, ensuring no one goes hungry.

Key Highlights of the Initiative

Extensive Reach

The food supply box initiative reaches thousands of households, making a significant impact in alleviating hunger and food insecurity across various regions in Egypt.

Community-Centered Approach

By working closely with local communities, Cairo Festival City Mall and Misr El Kheir Foundation ensure that aid is delivered effectively to those who need it most.

Sustained Impact

This ongoing initiative is designed to provide consistent support to struggling families, making a lasting impact on their quality of life.

Success Stories

Fatima’s Story: Fatima, a mother of three, received a food supply box, which allowed her family to have nutritious meals during a difficult period.

Ahmed’s Experience: Ahmed, who lost his job, was able to support his family thanks to the food box initiative, which provided much-needed relief.
