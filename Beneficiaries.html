Beneficiaries' Stories

Stories of Transformation and Hope

Misr El Kheir Foundation has been instrumental in transforming lives across Egypt through various programs. These initiatives, focused on education, healthcare, and social services, have helped numerous individuals achieve their dreams and improve their communities.

Educational Scholarships

MEK’s scholarship programs have enabled students from underserved regions to access higher education. Through financial assistance, students like Paules Zakhary have pursued ambitious careers and brought hope to their families. Paules, who aspired to work for NASA, credits MEK's support for making his educational journey possible. Many beneficiaries from Upper Egypt have also enrolled in prestigious institutions, like the American University in Cairo, thanks to MEK’s scholarships.

Healthcare Support

MEK has also provided critical healthcare support, establishing clinics and offering medical services in areas with limited access to healthcare. This initiative has brought health and well-being to countless individuals, enhancing the quality of life for many in rural and remote regions.

Social Services

Beyond education and healthcare, MEK has empowered communities through various social services. From building infrastructure to supporting sustainable livelihoods, MEK’s programs aim to foster resilience and create lasting change in Egypt’s most vulnerable communities.
